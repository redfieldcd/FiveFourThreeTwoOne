## Description

Feel overwhelmed or anxious? Take a moment to ground yourself.

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding guides you through a simple, proven mindfulness technique used by therapists worldwide. By focusing on your five senses, you gently bring your attention back to the present moment — one sense at a time.

HOW IT WORKS:
- 5 things you see
- 4 things you can touch
- 3 things you hear
- 2 things you smell
- 1 thing you taste

FEATURES:
- Guided grounding flow with a calming breathing exercise to start
- Voice or text input — speak your reflections or type them manually
- Real-time speech recognition with tap-to-confirm — speak, then tap to count each item
- Voice guidance with soothing audio prompts for each step
- Save and revisit past reflections to track your mindfulness journey
- Location tagging — remember where you found your calm
- Beautiful themes — choose from Default, Cosmic Indigo, or Sakura
- Ambient background music for a peaceful experience
- Gentle haptic feedback throughout the experience
- Record your own custom voice prompts
- Completely private — all data stays on your device

Whether you're managing anxiety, practicing mindfulness, or simply need a moment to pause, 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding is your pocket companion for finding calm anywhere, anytime.

No account needed. No ads. No tracking. Just breathe.

## App Review Notes (for Apple reviewers)

This app requires microphone and speech recognition permissions to function fully. To test:

1. Launch the app and tap "New Reflection" (+ button)
2. The app begins with a 3-second breathing countdown
3. You will be guided through 5 steps (See, Touch, Hear, Smell, Taste)
4. Each step allows voice input (microphone) or manual text entry
5. Grant microphone and speech recognition permissions when prompted
6. Location permission is optional — used to tag reflections with a place name
7. Completed reflections are saved and viewable from the home screen
8. Settings allow theme changes and custom voice prompt recording

No login or account is required. All data is stored locally on-device.
